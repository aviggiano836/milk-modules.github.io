Project activities

The MILK project is comprised of separate course modules, which are designed to meet a wide range of experience levels ranging from new programmers, to seasoned mobile developers. In the following section, we will briefly detail each activity.

Beginner

These activities are designed for students just learning about mobile development or accessibility.

  • Color blind safe accessibility (simple)
  • Keyboard navigation
  • Speech-based accessibility (simple)
  • UI element sizing
  • Video captions

Intermediate

The following activities are slightly more advanced.

  • Text to speech
  • Color blind safe accessibility
  • Speech-based accessibility

Advanced

The following activities are designed for students and developers who have a fair amount of experience with mobile development.

  • Navigation
  • Non-audio Notifications
